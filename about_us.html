About Us

We, at Minuscules, are a group of students from IISER Tirupati who are aiming to disseminate knowledge and awareness among people about the various species that make up Phylum Arthropoda. We aim to enlighten people about the distinguishing features, how to identify and the less talked-about importance of conservation of these little creatures that share existence with us on this beautiful planet. We are headed towards bringing the people with similar interests together, and developing a community that incorporates entomologist and entomophiles from across the globe.
